FAQ

What is the maximum weight the Razer Fujin can support?
The Razer Fujin can support up to 300lbs/136kg.
What is the ideal range of height the Razer Fujin can support?
The Razer Fujin can support between 65 inches/165 cm to 79 inches/200 cm.
Does the Razer Fujin come with a lumbar cushion?
The Razer Fujin features a lumbar support system that does not require an additional lumbar cushion for optimal support.
Does the Razer Fujin come with a head cushion?
The Razer Fujin does not feature any head support. If you prefer head support, you can purchase the optional Razer Fujin Headrest.
How do I keep the mesh fabric on the Razer Fujin clean?
Wiping down the mesh fabric on the Razer Fujin with a slightly damp cloth will help keep it dust and grime free.
How does the Razer Fujin compare to the Razer Iskur and Razer Enki?
The Razer Fujin uses mesh for those with the preference for more air circulation. The Razer Iskur and Razer Enki use a combination of high-density foam cushions and synthetic leather.
